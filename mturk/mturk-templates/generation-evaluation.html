You can visit The Onion website to check out some examples of headlines that can be on the page.

Note: You can identify a headline to have the potential to be on The Onion without necessarily finding it funny. For sentences that you deem incoherent, please rate it as not having the potential to be on The Onion.

Funniness

We trust your own intuition with whether or not a sentence is funny. You will give each sentence a score from 1 to 3 for how funny the sentence is. For sentences that are not coherent, you can give the score 1, meaning it is not funny.
